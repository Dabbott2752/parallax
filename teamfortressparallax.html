Team Fortress 2

Team Fortress 2, a 2007 video game masterpiece created by VALVe, is a first-person shooter that takes place in 1968. There is an endless customization of hats, weapon skins, unusuals, stranges, killstreaks, and many other effects. 9 class characters are introduced: Scout, the loud Boston boy. Soldier, the Patriotic American. Pyro, the mumbling pyromaniac. Demoman, the Drunk Scotsman. Heavy, the trigger happy Russian. Engineer, the genius Texan builder. Medic, the German Megalomaniac healer. Sniper, the New Zealand Head Hunter. And Spy, the Sneaky Backstabbing Frenchman.

BLU Team

BLU Team, otherwise known as "Builders League United", was founded by CEO Blutarch Mann. They specialize in construction operations, secretly controls half of the world's governments, and is one of the two protagonist factions in the video game Team Fortress 2 (2007).

RED Team

RED Team, otherwise known as "Reliable Excavation Demolition", was founded by CEO Redmond Mann. They specialize in demolition operations, secretly controls half of the world's governments, and is one of the two protagonist factions in the video game Team Fortress 2 (2007).

Mann Co.

Mann Co. is a weapon, gadget and cosmetic manufacturer in the video game Team Fortress 2 (2007). It was founded by Zepheniah Mann, a wealthy Englishman and father to Blutarch, Redmond, and Gray Mann. The ownership of Mann Co. started at Zepheniah Mann, Barnabas Hale, Saxton Hale, and Olivia Mann (current owner).
